Come Visit Us!

If you're looking for a cold beverage and a relaxing atmosphere then look no further. Located in the suburbs of Rochester, Normal Quirks has several options to suit your needs. There's a large indoor dining space that is great for year round enjoyment. When the weather is nice there's also an outdoor patio area where you can drink, eat, and relax until closing time. Oh, and most importantly, we are dog-friendly for those that are well behaved. Bring the dogs, kids, and anyone else you'd love to share a pint with. All our welcome here.

What's On Tap?

  • Flagship: IPA brewed with Citra, Mosaic, and Simcoe. Standard. Predictable. Delicious.
  • Flagship 2.0: Double IPA brewed with Citra, Mosaic, and Simcoe. Stronger. Boulder. Still Delicious.
  • The Dark Side: Stout brewed with Coffee and Vanilla. Think your morning cup of joe, but smoother and more exciting.
  • Ol' Reliable: Our take on a blonde ale because who doesn't love a classic.

Upcoming Events

Here at Normal Quirks we believe that beer is always better together.
Therefore, we try to put on a few events every week to help make that happen.

  • Brew Crew

    From 5-7pm every first Monday of the month. Come on down and get a first hand look at what goes into our brewing process. Due to safety precautions, you must be 21 or older to participate.

  • Live Music Fridays

    Live music begins at 8 and continues until 11. Weather permitting the music will be outside so dress accordingly.

  • Beer Mile Extravaganza

    If two of your favorite things are running and drinking then you're in luck. Saturday, July 1st will be the 5th annual beer mile. Rules and regulations can be found HERE. Please sign up by June 15th to secure your spot or just come on down to enjoy the festivities.

  • NFL Sundays

    We love the NFL as much as anyone. That's why we are open and showing all 1 o'clock games every Sunday. Happy hour goes from 12pm-2pm, and feel free to call about reserving a table with a good view of the game you're most interested in.
